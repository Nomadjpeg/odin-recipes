Tatertot Casserole



This casserole is a recipe that was made by my Grandmother in Law. It has been a great tradition in my family to make this every other week on wednesdays to make to the weekend.


Ingredients

  • Bag of frozen tater tots
  • 1lb of ground beef
  • Shredded cheese
  • Cream of mushrooom soup

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef in a skillet until brown
  2. in a baking pan place your frozen tots evenly throughout along with the cream of mushroom soup
  3. Preheat the ove to 350 degrees F
  4. Place cooked ground beef over the tatertot and soup mixture in the pan
  5. sprinkle shredded cheese over the top of the beef
  6. Cover the pan in tinfoil and place it in the oven for 1 hour
  7. After an hour remove the tinfoil cover and cook for an additonal 15 min
  8. ENJOY!
